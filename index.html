Minecraft The island

When a young man wakes up an a unfamiliar world, he quickly figures out key elements to his survival. Discovering new crafting skills, collecting resources and finding food supplies is all in a days work for this lone castaway. When least exepected, he finds out the secret power of the zombies, their fists. They beat down his house, set his island on fire and almost kill him numerous times. Its up to his courage, determination and cunning wits to pass his countless challenges.
